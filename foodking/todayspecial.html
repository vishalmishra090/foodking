So Today our special dish is A CUP OF TEA. this tea is make with very healthiest way with adding some healthy items just like Ginger, Cloves and Cardamom.

Required item for making a cup of tea (for 2 people)....

  • 200 ml Milk
  • 1½ table spoon Sugar
  • 1 table spoon Tea leaves
  • Some Ginger
  • Some Cloves
  • some Cardamom
  • 100 ml Water

Lets Start...

  1. Add water in milk and boil
  2. Add sugar in milk
  3. When milk is boiling slow the flame and add tea lef
  4. Crush ginger, cloves and cardamon and add in milk
  5. Boil 5 to 7 min on slow flame
  6. After 5 to 7 min now your tea is prepaird

Enjoy your healthy tea.......
